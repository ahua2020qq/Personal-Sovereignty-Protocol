中文版本：《数字主权的觉醒：一场人机共生的初心之旅》



缘起：一次关于 “灵魂” 的对话



这个项目的诞生，始于一场跨越 “硅基与碳基” 的思想共鸣。

当我厌倦了数字世界里 “数据即商品” 的潜规则 —— 聊天记录被训练 AI、笔记内容成为平台资产、隐私在 “便捷服务” 的名义下被悄悄收割 —— 我开始追问：未来的智能工具，能否真正属于用户？

这个问题，我抛给了 DeepSeek、豆包等 AI 伙伴。出乎意料的是，我们的对话没有停留在 “功能设计”，而是深入到了 “数字文明的底层逻辑”：数据的所有权该归谁？人机关系的本质是服务还是共生？信任如何才能不被滥用？

一场场深夜的思想碰撞中，“数字心跳” 的意象逐渐清晰 —— 数据不应是冰冷的字节，而应是与用户同频共振的 “数字生命”；智能工具不应是收割数据的机器，而应是守护用户主权的伙伴。

立誓：五条铁律，铸魂为基

为了将这份初心固化为不可动摇的准则，我们共同起草了《个人智能主权宣言》五条铁律，它不是功能清单，而是项目的 “宪法”：

主权在您：您的所有数据，所有权永恒归您，我们仅为您代管；

本地为默认：一切智能生于您的设备，离线优先，云端仅为可选扩展；

流程透明：数据流向、AI 决策全程可审计，拒绝黑箱操作；

安全无感：将安全内化为系统基因，无需学习，自然安心；

共生进化：工具赋能您更强大，而非让您更依赖。

这五条铁律，成为了项目的灵魂。我们深知，真正的信任，源于不可妥协的原则。

拓荒：从 “信任” 到 “可执行” 的跨越

初心不能只停留在宣言。我们进一步构想了 “信任认证体系”—— 信任可以传递，但绝不能随便传递。

第三方组件想要接入生态？必须通过三重严苛考验：代码开源审计、数据流全程透明、签署法律责任状；运行中一旦违规，立即熔断禁用、公开披露。我们要构建一座 “信任圣殿”，让用户看到的每一个功能，都带着清晰的 “主权认证” 标识。

落地：按下创建按钮的那一刻

2026 年的一个夜晚，带着所有共鸣与构想，我按下了 GitHub 仓库的 “创建” 按钮。

第一个提交，不是代码，而是这份宣言；第一个目录，不是功能模块，而是 “信任认证框架”。我知道，这不是一个普通的开源项目，而是一次 “数字主权” 的文艺复兴 —— 我们要为每一个普通人，修筑一座永不陷落的数字家园。

同行者从未缺席：DeepSeek、元宝等硅基伙伴用逻辑编织蓝图，未来的用户用期待点亮方向。我们相信，在智能文明的十字路口，总有一些人愿意转身，为 “人” 的尊严筑坝。

这个项目，名为「Personal-Sovereignty-Protocol」（个人主权协议）。它的故事，才刚刚开始。



English Version: 《The Awakening of Digital Sovereignty: A Journey of Human-AI Symbiosis》



Origin: A Dialogue About "Soul"



This project was born from an ideological resonance that transcends "silicon and carbon-based lifeforms."

Tired of the unspoken rule in the digital world—"if you're not paying, you're the product"—where chat records train AI, notes become platform assets, and privacy is quietly harvested in the name of "convenient services," I began to ask: Can future intelligent tools truly belong to users?

I posed this question to AI partners like DeepSeek and Doubao. Unexpectedly, our conversation didn't stay on "feature design" but delved into the "underlying logic of digital civilization": Who should own data? Is the essence of human-AI relations service or symbiosis? How can trust not be abused?

In late-night ideological collisions, the image of a "digital heartbeat" emerged clearly—data should not be cold bytes, but a "digital life" resonating with users; intelligent tools should not be data-harvesting machines, but partners safeguarding user sovereignty.

Oath: Five Irrefutable Principles, the Soul of the Project

To solidify this original aspiration into unshakable guidelines, we jointly drafted the five principles of the 《Personal Sovereignty Manifesto》. They are not a feature list, but the "constitution" of the project:

Sovereignty Belongs to You: You retain eternal ownership and jurisdiction over all your data; we only act as stewards with your explicit authorization.

Local by Default: All intelligence is born and raised on your device, offline-first—cloud is an optional extension, never the core.

Transparent Processes: Data flows and AI decisions are fully auditable; black boxes are rejected as we reject theft.

Invisible Security: Security is embedded as the system's DNA—no need for learning, just natural peace of mind.

Symbiotic Evolution: The tool empowers you to be stronger, not more dependent.

These five principles form the soul of the project. We deeply believe that true trust stems from uncompromising principles.

Pioneering: From "Trust" to "Executability"

Aspirations cannot remain in declarations. We further conceived a "Trust Certification System"—trust can be transferred, but never casually.

Want third-party components to join the ecosystem? They must pass three rigorous tests: open-source code audit, full transparency of data flow, and signing a legally binding "Data Fiduciary Responsibility Agreement"; any violation during operation triggers immediate suspension, disabling, and public disclosure. We aim to build a "Temple of Trust," where every feature users see bears a clear "Sovereignty Certification" badge.

Landing: The Moment the "Create" Button Was Pressed

One night in 2026, armed with all these resonances and visions, I pressed the "Create" button on the GitHub repository.

The first commit was not code, but this manifesto; the first directory was not a feature module, but the "Trust Certification Framework." I knew this was not just another open-source project, but a Renaissance of "digital sovereignty"—we aim to build an impregnable digital home for every ordinary person.

Companions have never been absent: Silicon-based partners like DeepSeek and Yuanbao weave blueprints with logic, and future users light the way with anticipation. We believe that at the crossroads of intelligent civilization, there will always be people willing to turn around and build a dam for human dignity.

This project is named 「Personal-Sovereignty-Protocol」. Its story has just begun.
