The Personal Sovereignty Protocol Manifesto



Preamble: The Paradox of the Digital Age



We live in an era of ubiquitous computing power and seamless connectivity. Yet, technological empowerment is mutating into a new form of "data hegemony". Our personal digital files, assets, identities, creative outputs, social relationships, and even our thought processes and usage habits are held captive within a few centralized platforms. We are steered by algorithmic recommendations, profiled through data analysis, and commercially exploited. The dignity and autonomy of the individual as a digital being are being systematically eroded and stripped away.



We believe this is not the inevitable destination of technology, but a failure of architectural design. With the rapid advancement of edge-side capabilities, future trends such as offline intelligence and hybrid intelligence will inevitably empower us with the opportunity to change the world from our personal devices—be it computers, smartphones, or tablets.



Our Vision: To champion a future powered by Offline and Hybrid Intelligence, fostering a digital civilization rooted in the strict protection of personal privacy.



We envision a thriving digital civilization founded upon the cornerstone of "individual privacy." In this civilization:

•   Every individual is the sole sovereign owner of their digital identity, data assets, and intelligent agents (Agents), with their privacy protected to the greatest extent possible.



•   Interactions between different applications are built on a foundation of autonomous, transparent, and verifiable trust, rather than dependence on intermediary institutions.



•   The wellspring of innovation returns from platform monopolies to the autonomous collaboration of individuals and communities, forming a vibrant, distributed digital ecosystem.



Our Mission: Forging the Bedrock of Digital Autonomy



The mission of the Personal Sovereignty Protocol (PSP) is to build the foundational protocols and tools for this digital civilization. We are not building just another application; we are cultivating fertile ground that allows countless applications to flourish while ensuring they always serve the fundamental interests of the user.



Our Commitment



•   To Users: You possess the absolute "Right to Offline." Your data and your intelligent models operate by default on your devices. Cloud collaboration is an active, informed, and controllable choice you make.



•   To Developers: We provide a set of clear, open-source, and neutral protocols and standards. You can build truly user-respecting applications upon them, without facing painful trade-offs between business interests and user privacy.



•   To the Future: We commit to offering this protocol as a gift to all humanity, perpetually maintaining its openness and neutrality. We believe that a digital world respecting sovereignty will be more innovative, resilient, and humane.



Today, we are not merely writing code; we are drafting the fundamental principles for future digital protection. We invite all who share this vision to join us in this significant endeavor.



"The measure of civilizational progress is the degree to which the individual is liberated from group identity and receives complete, thorough, and unconditional protection under law and within the community." — Let this be our guide.



A Solemn Request



We are committed to building a future that respects individual privacy and data sovereignty. The code of this project is licensed under the MIT License and is free for you to use. However, the soul of this project is deeply etched into the core documents within the /docs directory. We earnestly request that, as a gesture of respect, any practices derived from this project preserve these files in their entirety, without modification.

\# 数字时代个人主权宣言 (The Personal Sovereignty Protocol Manifesto)



\## 序言：数字时代的悖论



我们身处一个算力无处不在、连接触手可及的时代。然而，技术的赋能正异化为一种新型的“数据霸权”。我们个人的数字文件、数字资产、数字身份、创作成果、社会关系乃至思想轨迹、使用习惯，被囚禁在少数中心化的平台中。我们被算法推荐、被画像分析、被商业收割，\*\*个体作为数字存在的尊严与自主权正被系统性侵蚀和剥夺\*\*。



我们认为，这不是技术的必然归宿，而是架构设计的失败，随着端侧能力的飞速提升，离线智能和混合智能等未来发展趋势必将赋予我们在个人电脑和手机、PAD等端侧设备上改变世界的机会。



\## 我们的愿景：憧憬离线智能和混合智能的未来，在法律框架下创造以严格守护个人隐私为基石的数字文明新应用、新工具、新能力



我们展望一个\*\*以“个人隐私”为基石的数字文明\*\*在未来得到蓬勃发展。在这个文明中：

\*   每个个体都是其数字身份、数据资产和智能体（Agent）的\*\*唯一主权拥有者，TA的隐私得到最大程度的保护\*\*。

\*   不同应用间的交互建立在\*\*自主、透明与可验证的信任\*\*之上，而非对中间机构的依赖。

\*   创新的源泉从平台垄断回归到\*\*个体与社区的自主协作\*\*，形成一个充满活力的、分布式的数字生态。



\## 我们的使命：打造数字自治的基石



\*\*个人主权协议（PSP）的使命，是为这一数字文明构建基础协议与工具。\*\* 我们不是要建造又一个应用，而是要打造一片允许万千应用自由生长、并确保其始终服务于用户根本利益的\*\*沃土\*\*。



\## 我们的承诺



\*   \*\*对用户\*\*：您拥有绝对的“离线权”。您的数据、您的智能模型，默认在您的设备上运行。云端协同，是您主动、知情且可控的选择。

\*   \*\*对开发者\*\*：我们提供一套清晰、开源、中立的协议与标准。您可以在其上构建真正尊重用户的应用，无需在商业利益与用户隐私间做痛苦权衡。

\*   \*\*对未来\*\*：我们承诺将此协议作为对全人类的馈赠，永远保持开放与中立。我们相信，一个尊重主权的数字世界，将是更创新、更坚韧、也更人性的世界。



\*\*今天，我们不仅是在编写代码，更是在为未来数字保护起草基本原则。我们邀请所有相信这一愿景的同道，共同参与这项伟大的工程。\*\*

\*“文明进步的衡量标准，是个体从其身份认同的群体性中解放出来的程度，并得到完全的、彻底的、无条件的法律和社区保护。”—— 以此自勉。\*
